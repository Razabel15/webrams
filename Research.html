Digital Twin for Autonomous Navigation Safety.

Developing a real-time risk assessment framework utilizing digital twin technology to monitor and predict collision risks for autonomous surface vessels (ASVs) in dense shipping lanes like the Malacca Strait.

Industry Collaboration
Partner: PT Pertamina Hulu Energi

Offshore Pipeline Integrity Management.

Implementation of a dynamic Risk-Based Inspection (RBI) methodology for aging subsea pipelines, integrating historical inspection data with advanced corrosion degradation models to optimize maintenance scheduling and extend asset lifecycle.

Project Impacts:

  • check_circle 30% reduction in unnecessary inspections
  • check_circle Enhanced API 581 compliance
  • check_circle ALARP justification for continuous operation
