PUI-KEKAL.

Maritime Safety and Marine Installation.

A Center of Excellence at the university level owned by Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS) Surabaya, dedicated to advancing maritime safety and technology in Indonesia.

Research Experience.

The journey of Maritime Safety and Marine Installation research from TKT 1 to TKT 8.

Insentif Ristek

LBE

2018 • TKT 8

Impact & Engagement

Highlighted Activities.

Showcasing our continuous efforts in collaboration, implementation, and public outreach to elevate maritime safety standards.

Implementation

AISITS Implementation for Upstream Oil & Gas

Socialization and implementation of the AISITS application as a vital safety support tool for upstream oil and gas operations. This strategic event was attended by officials from SKK MIGAS and KKKS.

Global Partnership

AISITS collaboration with Pelindo III in building AI Based MAnuvering plan

Receiving a distinguished visit from IHI Corporation representatives. The meeting focused on exploring and establishing potential collaborative research and industrial partnerships with PUI-KEKAL.

Workshop

Smart Buoy collaboration with Panasonic & Disnav surabaya

Hosting a technical workshop alongside the UK Embassy to discuss the Prosperity Fund. The initiative aims to significantly develop and enhance Vocational Education within the Maritime Field.

Exhibition

Maritime Booth exibition in Grand city surabaya

Proudly opening and operating a dedicated booth at the Hakteknas (National Technology Awakening Day) 2018 Exhibition in Pekanbaru, showcasing our innovations to the public and stakeholders.
