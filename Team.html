Meet The Researchers

A multidisciplinary team composed of 90% PhD holders, combining decades of academic excellence with deep industrial experience in marine safety and reliability.

school article

Prof. Dr. Ketut Buda Artana, S.T., M.Sc.

Graduated from Bachelor Degree of Marine Engineering Department, Faculty of Marine Technology, ITS in 1993. He continued his study in Master Degree Program in the Department of Marine Technology, University of Newcastle Upon Tyne, UK and completed in September 1997. Then, He graduated from Doctoral Degree Program of Marine Engineering in Kobe University of Mercantile Marine, Japan in 2003. In 2010, he awarded Professorship in Reliability and Safety of Marine System. He experienced in leading risk assessment of some oil and gas facilities belong to several companies in Surabaya, Papua, and East Kalimantan such as PHE-WMO, Petronas Carigali, HCML, Kangean Energy Indonesia, MCL, BP, Chevron, Eni Muara Bakau B.V., etc.
mail ketutbuda@its.ac.id

Prof. Dr. I Made Ariana, S.T., M.T.

Graduated from Bachelor Degree of Marine Engineering Department, Faculty of Marine Technology, ITS Surabaya in 1995. He took Master Degree Program in the Marine Technology Department, ITS Surabaya in 2000. Then, he was graduated for Doctoral Degree Program in Marine Engineering, Faculty of Maritime Sciences and Technology, Kobe University, Japan in 2008. His academic main field is marine internal combustion engine, but he quiet involved in consequence modelling of several risk assessment process, particularly for oil and gas facilities. He has experienced in risk assessment process of some oil and gas facilities belong to several companies.
mail ariana@its.ac.id

A.A. Bagus Dinariyana, S.T., MES, Ph.D.

Graduated from Bachelor Degree Marine Engineering Department, Faculty of Marine Technology, ITS Surabaya in 1999. He took Master Degree Program in the Institute of Environmental Studies, Graduate School of Frontier Sciences, The University of Tokyo, Japan in 2003. Then, He graduated from Doctoral Degree for Human and Engineered Environment, Graduate School of Frontier Sciences, The University of Tokyo, Japan, in 2008. His scope of research are Marine System Safety and Reliability, Risk Assessment, Maintenance Management, Optimization and Simulation in Marine Applications.
mail dinariyana@yahoo.com

Dr. Eng. Kriyo Sambodho, S.T., M.Eng.

Graduated from Bachelor Degree of Offshore Engineering Department, Faculty of Marine Technology, ITS in 1997. He took Master and Doctoral Degree Program in the Department of Civil and Earth Resources Engineering, Kyoto University and graduated consecutively in 2006 and 2009. His main field of expertise is geo technics, particularly of coastal and offshore, coastal processes, hydro-oceanography, and disaster management of coastal area. For public services, he experienced with geo technics assessment with PHE-WMO, Petronas, etc.
mail k-sambodho@oe.its.ac.id

Dr. Eng. Dhimas Widhi H., S.T., M.Sc.

Graduated for bachelor degree from the Department of Marine Engineering, Faculty of Marine Technology, ITS Surabaya in 2009. He took Master and Doctor Degree Program in the Graduate School of Maritime Science and Technology, Kobe University, and has graduated in 2011 and 2014, consecutively. On 2011 he achieved "Best Paper Award" for Honorable Mention Award from IEEE Conference on "Industrial Engineering and Engineering Management". His main field of expertise is system dynamics and its application.
mail dhimas@ne.its.ac.id

Fadilla I. Prastyasari, S.T., M.Sc., Ph.D.

Graduated from Bachelor Degree of Marine Engineering Department, Faculty of Marine Technology, ITS, Surabaya in 2014. She took Master Degree program in Marine Engineering at Newcastle University, UK and graduated in 2017. She continued her Doctoral degree in Kyushu University, Japan. Her field of study was deeply emphasizing the safety of navigation. Currently Dr. Fadilla is a lecturer in Marine Engineering Department of ITS Surabaya, and actively conducting research to improve the safety of maritime operation.
mail fadilla@its.ac.id
