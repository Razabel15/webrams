About RAMS Lab

Safeguarding the Future of Complex Operations.

Located within the Department of Marine Engineering at the Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS), the RAMS Laboratory is a premier research facility dedicated to mitigating risks in high-stakes environments.

We bridge the gap between academic theory and industrial application, providing robust models, predictive analytics, and safety frameworks that ensure critical infrastructures operate without failure.

Exceptional Human Resources

Supported by a team where 90% are PhD holders, possessing 10-25 years of hands-on experience in public services and engineering.

Multidisciplinary Expertise

Integrating Marine Technology, Civil, Chemical, Safety, Instrumentation, Electrical, Geotechnic, and Environmental Engineering.
