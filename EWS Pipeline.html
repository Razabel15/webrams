Problem Context

Navigating the Dense Traffic of APBS.

The West Surabaya Navigation Channel (APBS) experiences extreme vessel density directly above critical subsea gas pipelines. Human error and uncontrolled anchoring pose catastrophic risks to this energy infrastructure.
