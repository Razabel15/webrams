Capabilities.

Advanced reliability, availability, maintainability, and safety analysis for complex engineering systems.

The RAMS Laboratory provides integrated analytical, computational, and experimental capabilities to assess, predict, and improve the reliability, availability, maintainability, and safety of critical systems across their full lifecycle.

Core Capability

Reliability Engineering.

We quantify and improve system reliability at component, subsystem, and system levels using advanced statistical modeling.

Methods

  • Reliability block diagrams (RBD)
  • Failure rate modeling
  • Fault Tree Analysis (FTA)

Outputs

  • MTBF & Failure probability
  • Reliability allocation
  • Criticality ranking
Core Capability

Availability & Resilience.

Assessment of system uptime, redundancy, and operational resilience under normal and degraded conditions.

Methods

  • Availability modeling
  • Redundancy analysis
  • Resilience assessment

Outputs

  • Availability metrics (Ao, Ai)
  • Downtime estimation
  • Redundancy optimization
Core Capability

Safety & Risk Engineering.

Comprehensive safety assessment for high-risk and safety-critical systems using quantitative and qualitative methods.

Methods

  • HAZID, HAZOP
  • Quantitative Risk (QRA)
  • Bow-Tie analysis

Outputs

  • Risk matrices
  • Mitigation strategies
  • ALARP evaluation
Core Capability

Maintainability Engineering.

Optimization of maintenance strategies to reduce downtime and lifecycle costs.

Methods

  • MTTR modeling
  • RCM (Reliability Centered)
  • Lifecycle analysis

Outputs

  • Optimized schedules
  • Cost–benefit analysis
  • Spare parts planning
