Frequently Asked Questions.

How can my company collaborate with RAMS Laboratory?
add
We welcome industry partnerships through direct consultancy, joint research grants, and specialized training programs. Please fill out the contact form selecting "Industry Consultancy" and our lead researcher will contact you to schedule a meeting.
Are your laboratory facilities open for student testing?
add
Yes, active ITS Marine Engineering students working on thesis projects related to reliability and safety can request access. Submit a formal request letter signed by your supervisor to the lab administrator.
Where exactly is the lab located within the campus?
add
We are located within the Department of Marine Engineering main building complex, 1st floor, East Wing. Ask the department front desk for exact directions upon your arrival.
